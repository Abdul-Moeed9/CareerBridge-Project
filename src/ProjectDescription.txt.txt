1. Project Overview
CareerBridge is a desktop-based application designed to connect job seekers with companies
offering internships and jobs in Pakistan. The platform provides access to authentic job listings by
integrating Rozee.pk RSS feeds and jobdataapi.com, ensuring job seekers always see the latest
opportunities. Companies can post verified internships, manage applications, and gain insights into
candidate quality through analytics dashboards. Job seekers can compare their profiles with job
requirements using AI powered match scoring, filter opportunities based on location, role,
experience, CGPA, and stipend, and view top-ranked job recommendations.
2. Motivation
Finding relevant internships and entry-level jobs in Pakistan is often difficult for job seekers due
to scattered information, outdated listings, and lack of guidance. Similarly, companies struggle to
reach qualified candidates and filter through large numbers of applications. CareerBridge is
motivated by the need to provide a centralized platform where job seekers can access fresh
opportunities, receive intelligent guidance, and companies can efficiently recruit candidates. By
combining live job feeds, AI-based matching, and interactive dashboards, the system addresses
key challenges faced by both job seekers and employers.
3. Objectives
Provide job seekers with job and internship listings from verified sources.
Implement filtering based on location, skills, CGPA, role, experience, and stipend.
Enable profile-to-job recommendations and matching for job seekers.
Enable whitelisted companies to post job offerings and internships.
4. Expected Outcomes
Job seekers browse daily job updates, receive match scores with skill gaps, and apply selectively
using filters for location, stipend/salaries, and experience. Companies view applications, rank top
candidates via weighted scoring, and access analytics (avg CGPA, match distribution).
5. Project Scope
In-Scope:
Integration of job listings via Rozee.pk RSS feeds and jobdataapi.com.
Registration, login, and profile creation for job seekers.
A pre-approved whitelist of companies that can register and access dashboards for
posting and managing internships/jobs.
AI-powered job comparison and skill-gap analysis using Weka ML.
Advanced job filters: location, experience, role category, CGPA, stipend.
Out-of-Scope:
• Messaging systems between job seekers and companies.
• Interview scheduling or live video interviews.
• Public company registration.
6. Tools and Technologies
Frontend: Java and JavaFX for dual-mode dashboards (Job seeker/Company)
Backend: MySQL for jobs, internships, and applications.
APIs: Rozee.pk RSS Feed and jobdataapi.com for real-time job updates.
AI & ML: Weka library for AI match scoring and weighted candidate ranking.
